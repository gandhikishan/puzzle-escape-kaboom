Bombs & Puzzles — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

Bombs & Puzzles is a free logic puzzle game for ages 3 and above. This policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

Information we collect

  • Game progress — stored only on your device (current stage, settings).
  • Analytics — if enabled, Firebase Analytics collects anonymous usage events (e.g. stages started/cleared) to improve the game.
  • Crash reports — if enabled, Firebase Crashlytics collects crash logs to fix bugs.
  • Advertising — Google AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad interaction data to show ads. The app is configured as child-directed with maximum content rating G and without personalized ads for children.

How we use information

Data is used solely to operate the game, show advertisements, measure performance, and fix crashes. We do not sell personal information.

Children

The game is designed for a general audience including children aged 3+. Ad requests are tagged child-directed and limited to family-safe (G-rated) content.

Third-party services

  • Google Privacy Policy (AdMob, Firebase)

Your choices

You can reset game progress in Settings. You may limit ad tracking in your device settings.
