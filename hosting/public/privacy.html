Privacy Policy — Puzzle Escape - Kaboom

Effective date: June 10, 2026  •  Last updated: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Puzzle Escape - Kaboom (the “App”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use it. The App is a free, offline-playable logic puzzle game intended for a general audience, including children. We are committed to protecting your privacy and to complying with Google Play's Families policy, the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

By downloading or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Summary

  • We do not require an account, sign-in, or any personal details to play.
  • Your game progress and settings are stored only on your device.
  • We show ads through Google AdMob, configured as child-directed with non-personalized ads and a maximum content rating of G (family-safe).
  • We may use Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config) to understand aggregate, anonymous usage and to fix crashes.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

2. Information we collect

CategoryWhatWhyStored where
Game data Current stage, best stage reached, sound setting, hint balance and daily-hint date. To save your progress and preferences. On your device only (local storage).
Advertising identifiers Device advertising ID (AAID) and limited ad-interaction/diagnostic data. To serve and measure ads via Google AdMob. Processed by Google.
Usage analytics Anonymous, aggregated events such as stage started/cleared, app opens, and approximate device/app info. To understand how the game is played and improve it. Processed by Google Firebase.
Crash diagnostics Crash logs, device model, OS version, and app state at the time of a crash. To detect and fix bugs and stability issues. Processed by Google Firebase (Crashlytics).

We do not knowingly collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, precise location, contacts, photos, or microphone/camera data.

3. Advertising

The App displays advertisements (including interstitial and rewarded video ads) provided by Google AdMob. To comply with policies for apps that may be used by children, ad requests are tagged as child-directed (“tag for child-directed treatment”), request non-personalized ads, and are limited to a maximum content rating of G (suitable for general/family audiences).

Rewarded ads are optional: you may choose to watch a short video to earn extra hints or lives. Watching is always your choice.

Learn more about how Google uses data from partner apps: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

4. How we use information

We use the information described above only to:

  • operate and save your progress within the game;
  • display and measure family-safe advertisements;
  • measure aggregate performance and engagement; and
  • diagnose and fix crashes and technical problems.

We do not use the information to build advertising profiles of children and we do not sell personal information.

5. Children's privacy

The App is suitable for ages 3 and up and is designed to comply with Google Play's Families policy and applicable children's privacy laws (including COPPA and GDPR-K). Advertising is configured for child-directed treatment and non-personalized delivery. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children beyond what is described here. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us (see Section 10) and we will promptly delete it.

6. Third-party services

The App relies on the following Google services, each governed by Google's own privacy terms:

  • Google AdMob — advertising.
  • Google Firebase Analytics — anonymous usage analytics.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics — crash reporting.
  • Google Firebase Remote Config — feature/configuration tuning.

See the Google Privacy Policy.

7. App permissions

The App requests only the permissions needed to show ads and report crashes:

  • INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — to load advertisements and send anonymous analytics/crash data.
  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID — to access the advertising identifier for ad delivery and measurement.

8. Data retention

On-device game data remains until you reset progress within the App or uninstall it. Data processed by Google (ads, analytics, crash reports) is retained according to Google's policies.

9. Your choices & rights

  • Reset progress: use Settings → Reset progress inside the App.
  • Limit ad tracking: in your device settings you can reset or delete your advertising ID and opt out of ad personalization (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads).
  • Remove all local data: uninstall the App or clear its storage in your device settings.
  • GDPR/CCPA: depending on your region, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.
